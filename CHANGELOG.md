
## Changelog

### v1.0.0 (Latest)

- **16 Device Types Supported**:
  - Switch, Light, Dimmable Switch
  - Blinds, Garage Door
  - Doorbell (with events)
  - Fan, Smart Lock
  - Speaker, TV (media players)
  - Thermostat, Window AC Unit (climate)
  - Air Quality Sensor, Temperature Sensor
  - Contact Sensor, Motion Sensor (binary sensors)
- **Alert System**: SinricPro alerts as persistent notifications
- **Event Automation**: Fire `sinricpro_alert` events
- **Enhanced Climate**: Fan mode support for AC units, ECO mode
- **Comprehensive Sensors**: Air quality (PM1/PM2.5/PM10), Temperature, Humidity
- **Binary Sensors**: Contact and Motion detection
- **Media Player**: Full TV and Speaker support with playback controls
- **Smart State Management**: Pending state with SSE confirmation across all devices
